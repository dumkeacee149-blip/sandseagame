Chart Your First Voyage

1 · Pick & Sail

Pick your captain — five heroes, one helm. No sign-up: your voyage saves right in the browser. Then set sail: WASD to steer, E to go ashore.

2 · Trade & Outfit

Dates buy cheap at the Oasis, sell sweet at Saltcrest. Every quest pays gold; every profit funds sails, cargo holds and hull at the shipwright.

3 · Risk & Claim

The far route to Duneskull pays triple — through leviathan territory. Survive it, buy the map, and open the relic vault.

Life on the Sandsea

Trade Routes

Three ports, four goods, honest price gaps. Near routes pay steady; far routes pay bold captains.

The Shipwright

Sails, cargo hold, hull — three upgrade paths. Sail L2 is the line where you outrun the beast.

The Leviathan

It patrols the deep sand between you and the richest spice run. It bites, you lose cargo. Plan around it — or through it.

The Relic Vault

A map sold only at the most dangerous outpost leads to the vault beneath the Sunken Ruins. Legends retire rich.
